asg0/ - a directory with files for assignment 0

main/ - directory with main code/shaders/html/etc.

asg0.js - javascript file associated with asg0.html
asg0.html - a web file that takes two vectors that takes up to 2 vectors, draws them with a red and blue line, and supports the following:

add 2 vectors, drawing a green vector third vector
subtract 2 vectors, drawing a green third vector
multiply 2 vectors with a scalar, drwaing 2 green vectors of product
divide 2 vectors with a scalar, drwaing 2 green vectors of quotient
find the magnitude of up to 2 vectors, displays in console
normalize two vectors, drawing their normalizations in 2 green vectors
find angle between two vectors, displaying the degree in the console
find the area of the triangle created with 2 vectors, displays area in console.
